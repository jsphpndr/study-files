Assignments

HTML Table

Ungraded

Due: Sat, Feb 13, 2021 12:00PM

Students are to prepare a table in HTML with no less than 3 rows (inclusive of headings) and 2 columns of data. Entries are at students’ discretion.

Blog Post — HTML

Graded

Due: Sat, Feb 27, 2021 12:00PM

Students will create a blog post in HTML. Students must utilize all headings (h1-h6), a minimum of five paragraphs and at least one image. Students may — and should, where possible — also include other relevant information pertinent to posts and articles like author name, summaries and dates. HTML must be semantic.
